## If We Have Four of a Kind:

* **+1 point**:
  The joker becomes the **fifth card** to make it a **five of a kind**.

---

## If We Have Three of a Kind:

* **+2 points**:
  Joker becomes a **fourth matching card** → **Four of a Kind**
* **+3 points**:
  Joker becomes **two matching cards** (if more than one joker) → **Five of a Kind**

---

## If We Have Two of a Kind:

### Case 1: Another Pair Exists (Two Pair)

* **+2 points**:
  Joker turns it into a **Full House**

### Case 2: No Second Pair

Depending on the number of jokers:

| Jokers | Result          | Points |
| ------ | --------------- | ------ |
| 1      | Three of a Kind | +2     |
| 2      | Four of a Kind  | +4     |
| 3      | Five of a Kind  | +5     |

---

## If We Have a High Card:

Depending on the number of jokers:

| Jokers | Result          | Points |
| ------ | --------------- | ------ |
| 1      | One Pair        | +1     |
| 2      | Three of a Kind | +3     |
| 3      | Four of a Kind  | +5     |
| 4      | Five of a Kind  | +6     |
